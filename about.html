    • About Liquid Designs

    Came To be in 2017, offering services through facebook and advertised in the local area of Oklahoma. It grew to having clients in North Carolina, California, Texas, Louisiana, Oklahoma, and Florida. Working with popular frameworks and the open source freedom of the most popular ones that are used today, Liquid Designs created their own framework.

    With a book of 300 clients, 20 independent contractors, and an endless amount of designs coming through, Liquid keeps on growing. The team is made of independent web developers and the founder of Liquid Designs. No matter how big or how small the project is, no one hardly ever gets turned away.
